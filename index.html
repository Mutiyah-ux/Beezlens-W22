About

Beezlens is a visual discovery engine for finding course contents based on college and university reviews, to explore hidden attractions and low-cost accommodations, and to inspire the world with your ideas, knowledge, style inspiration, and more.

With Beezlens you'll discover contents and pin ideas to build meaningful connections based on your search history, and recent activity to get your community running with moderation.

Create

Beezlens servers are organized into topic-based channels where you can collaborate, share, and just talk about your day without judgement.

Define your moment from few to a fandom!

With Beezlens, you can easily belong to a school club, an activity group, or a worldwide community where you and a handful of friends can spend time together in-person or remotely.

Home feed

With billions of recommendations to choose from on Beezlens, you'll always find ideas to spark inspiration. Use the search bar to discover ideas, people and trends. Explore suggested topics or search for topics of your own.

In your homefeed friends and connections in your server can see you’re around and instantly pop in to talk without having to call.
